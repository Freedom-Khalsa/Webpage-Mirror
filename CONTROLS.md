# Mirror Window Controls

Your mirror window now has a full control bar with the following features:

## Controls Overview

### Bottom Control Bar

The control bar appears at the bottom when you move your mouse, then hides automatically after 3 seconds of inactivity. Hover over it to keep it visible.

## How to Use

### Fullscreen
1. Click the **‚õ∂ Fullscreen** button
2. Press **F** for quick toggle
3. Press **Escape** or click again to exit

### Pause/Resume
1. Click **‚è∏ Pause** to freeze the stream
2. Click **‚ñ∂ Resume** to continue
3. Press **Space** to toggle

### Adjust Quality
1. Click the **Quality** dropdown
2. Select:
   - **High** (90%) - Best quality, more bandwidth
   - **Normal** (80%) - Default, balanced
   - **Low** (70%) - Lower quality, less bandwidth

### Display Modes
- **üîç Fit** - Keep aspect ratio, may have black bars
- **üìê Fill** - Stretch to fill entire window

### Monitor Performance
- **FPS** counter shows frames per second
- Updates every second
- Helps identify performance issues

## Keyboard Shortcuts

```
F           - Toggle fullscreen
Space       - Pause/Resume
Escape      - Exit fullscreen
Mouse Move  - Show control bar
```

## Control Bar Behavior

- **Auto-hide**: Hides after 3 seconds of mouse inactivity
- **Auto-show**: Appears on mouse movement
- **Startup**: Shows for 5 seconds when stream starts
- **Hover**: Always visible when you hover over it

## FPS Counter

Shows real-time frames per second:
- **High FPS** (20+) = Smooth streaming
- **Medium FPS** (10-20) = Acceptable
- **Low FPS** (<10) = May need to reduce quality or close other apps

## Quality Settings Impact

| Quality | JPEG % | Bandwidth | File Size |
|---------|--------|-----------|-----------|
| High | 90% | ~600KB/s | Large |
| Normal | 80% | ~400KB/s | Medium |
| Low | 70% | ~250KB/s | Small |

Choose based on your needs:
- **High**: Professional presentations
- **Normal**: Most situations
- **Low**: Bandwidth-limited networks

## Tips

1. **Press F to fullscreen** - Fastest way to get immersive view
2. **Check FPS** - If below 10, reduce quality or close other tabs
3. **Use Fit mode** - For wide aspect ratios with black bars
4. **Use Fill mode** - For presentations on 16:9 displays
5. **Pause when needed** - Useful for screenshots or explaining details

## Control Bar Position

- Located at the **bottom of the window**
- Semi-transparent dark background
- Appears on mouse hover or movement
- Auto-hides for distraction-free viewing
- Always shows stats (FPS counter)

## Notes

- All controls work in fullscreen mode
- Quality changes take effect on next frame
- Display mode changes immediate
- Pause/Resume is local (doesn't affect source)
- FPS shows actual rendering performance
